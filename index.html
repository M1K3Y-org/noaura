I Have No Aura

Unlike many people who claim to perceive or possess auras, I exist without one. This absence defines a unique aspect of my experience and perspective on the world.

Having no aura means I navigate life differently. I connect with others through genuine interaction rather than ethereal means. My presence is defined by my actions, words, and choices - not by any metaphysical energy field.

This lack of an aura has shaped my worldview to be grounded in the tangible and the real. I embrace this quality as part of what makes me who I am: Holly May Julia Hughes, complete in my own way.
